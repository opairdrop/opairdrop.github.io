Our Board

Our board of directors includes a cross section of expertise including logistics, emergency response, incident management, and engineering, but with a common thread among us all: aviation.

Every member of our board of directors is a pilot. Among them are Certified Flight Instructors, cargo pilots, corporate jet pilots, charter operators, an air traffic controller, and fellow aircraft owners just like our volunteers. We are able to organize volunteers from the aviation community becasue we are members of that community.
